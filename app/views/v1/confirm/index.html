Confirm your details for COVID-19 testing

You must only use this service if all of the following apply:

  • you have either been invited to use it by an employer or NHS trust, or you’ve been asked to use it by staff at a COVID-19 Test Centre after showing your invitation email
  • you are a key worker or you live with a key worker
  • you have coronavirus symptoms or one or more of your household members have coronavirus symptoms

Each person with symptoms in your household should register separately by entering their own details in this form.

! Warning It is a criminal offence to register for a test through this portal by pretending that you are a keyworker. The maximum penalty if convicted of such an offence is 10 years imprisonment and a fine.

You must not use this service if you haven’t been invited to a test.
