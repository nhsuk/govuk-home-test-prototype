Registration complete

We’ve sent you a text message from COVIDTest to confirm your registration.

There may be a delay of up to 30 minutes to receive your text message.

If you’re registered whilst at the test centre

Drive through as instructed by the security assistant and signs. If the text message doesn’t arrive or you need help, honk your horn twice.

If you’re not at the test centre yet

Please open the link in the text message when instructed to do so after you arrive at the test centre.

If you’ve already had your test and left the test centre

  1. Open the text message you received from COVIDTest and tap on the link in the message
  2. When prompted, please enter your date of birth, name and mobile phone number to confirm that it’s you
  3. Please scan the barcode attached on your TEST REGISTRATION CARD. Tap the ‘Scan my barcode’ button and point your phone camera at the barcode. The scanning should happen instantaneously
  4. If you are unable to scan your barcode, please enter the barcode in the box below and then re-enter. Tap the ‘Confirm’ button when you have submitted your barcode
  5. Your test is now linked to you and you will get your results delivered to this mobile number.

If you’ve already had your test and left the test centre

Please follow the instructions included with your testing kit.

If you experience issues with registration and scanning please call the HELPDESK (open 9am to 6pm) on 0203 514 3817. We are not able to answer medical queries or give you information about your result.
